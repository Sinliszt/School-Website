Campus Life

Library and Labs

Our Resource center is equipped with the latest books, periodicals & research reports.
Chrysalis High has labs for Biology, Chemistry, Physics & Maths for practical learning of the concepts. All labs are designed as per the specifications & requirement of the ICSE board.

Classrooms

All classrooms are well lit and ventilated. The elevation of the building is kept vibrant & bright to make it look welcoming for all our children.
Chrysalis High adheres to all safety measures defined by various agencies. Fire systems are installed at all key points. Fire drill is carried out twice in a year.

Cafeteria

In order to inculcate good eating habits and enhance bonding between a teacher and students, we encourage students and teacher of each grade to sit together and eat.
Chrysalis High serves only vegetarian meals inside the premises.
